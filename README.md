## 📋 C-DAC C-CAT Exam Pattern at a Glance
To crack the **CDAC C-CAT entrance exam**, understanding the structural blueprint and marking scheme is critical.

Section | Questions | Duration | Topics & Syllabus Covered
--- | --- | --- | ---
📗 **Section A** | 50 | 1 hour | English, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, Computer Fundamentals, Programming Concepts
📘 **Section B** | 50 | 1 hour | C Language, Data Structures (DSA), C++ OOP, Operating Systems (OS), Networking, Big Data, AI
📙 **Section C** | 50 | 1 hour | Digital Electronics, Computer Architecture, Microprocessors
**Total** | **150** | **3 hours** | **Maximum Score: 450 marks**

### 🎯 C-CAT Marking Scheme
*   **✅ Correct Answer:** +3 marks
*   **❌ Wrong Answer:** −1 mark (Negative Marking)
*   **⬜ Skipped Question:** 0 marks

⚠️ **Which sections do I need to attempt?**
*   **CS / IT / MCA students** → Section A + Section B (Fully covered in this repo)
*   **Electronics / Electrical students** → Section A + Section C

## 🧠 Why This AI-Driven Learning Framework Works
Traditional **CDAC study materials** encourage passive reading. This interactive prompt system utilizes active recall and spaced repetition:

❌ Traditional C-CAT Coaching | ✅ This Active Learning Method
--- | ---
Reading dry textbooks and notes passively | AI model actively teaches a concept and expects your interactive response
Attempting generic online MCQs blindly | Attempting targeted MCQs only after conceptual clarity is verified
Checking answer keys at the very end | Receives instant corrective feedback + hyper-deep architectural explanations
Unaware of structural weak points and gaps | AI engine purposefully cross-questions you to expose and patch learning gaps
Forgetting data models after a single pass | Socratic dialogue forces deep active recall for long-term memory encoding
